# AccessPrep AI - 3 Minute Demo Script

## 0:00 - 0:25 Opening
Hello everyone. We are Inclusive Intelligence Lab, and our team tagline is AI for Every Ability. Today we are presenting AccessPrep AI, an AI-powered Decision Intelligence platform for visually impaired students preparing for exams like TNPSC, Railway, SSC, Banking, and UPSC.

## 0:25 - 0:55 Problem
Many visually impaired aspirants receive study material as scanned PDFs, photos, or inaccessible notes. Even when OCR is available, they still need explanations, follow-up support, practice questions, and a realistic study plan. The preparation journey becomes fragmented and exhausting.

## 0:55 - 1:35 Upload and OCR
In the Upload page, the student uploads an image or PDF. AccessPrep AI previews the document, then extracts text using Google Vision OCR when credentials are available, with Gemini Vision as an AI fallback. The extracted text becomes accessible and reusable across the app.

## 1:35 - 2:10 AI Tutor
Now we move to AI Tutor. The student can ask Gemini to explain the concept, summarize it, extract key points, or generate five MCQs with answers. The chat interface supports follow-up questions, so learning becomes conversational instead of one-way.

## 2:10 - 2:40 Decision Intelligence
On the Study Recommendation page, the student enters exam, days remaining, daily study hours, and current score. AccessPrep AI combines this profile with learning evidence to recommend weak topics, the next chapter, revision topics, a seven-day study plan, and a confidence level.

## 2:40 - 3:00 Impact and Close
Finally, any explanation or plan can be converted to speech using gTTS. AccessPrep AI brings OCR, Computer Vision, Gemini, voice-first accessibility, and decision intelligence into one inclusive preparation assistant. Our goal is simple: competitive exam preparation that works for every ability.
